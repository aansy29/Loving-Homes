About Us

At Loving Homes, we are passionate about providing high-quality care for dogs in a safe, comfortable and welcoming environment. Our dog hotel is designed to feel like a home away from home, ensuring every pet receives the attention, comfort and care they deserve while their owners are away. We offer a range of services including premium boarding, daily care, play areas, and personalised routines tailored to each dog’s needs, personality and energy level. Our trained team is dedicated to maintaining a secure and loving space where dogs can relax, play and feel at ease. At Loving Homes, we prioritise trust, wellbeing and happiness, giving owners peace of mind knowing their pets are in caring hands.
