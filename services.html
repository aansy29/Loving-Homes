Our Services

At Loving Homes, we provide a range of professional dog care services designed to ensure your pet’s comfort, safety and happiness. From short daytime stays to extended boarding experiences, each service is carefully planned to meet the unique needs of every dog. Our trained staff, high-quality facilities and personalised care approach ensure that every pet receives attention, exercise and relaxation in a secure and welcoming environment.

The Services We Offer

Dog Boarding

Safe and comfortable overnight stays with full care and supervision.

Daycare

Fun and active daytime care for dogs while owners are busy.

Grooming & Spa

Professional grooming services to keep dogs clean and healthy.

Training

Basic obedience and behavioural training for well-mannered pets.

Veterinary Care

24-hour access to professional veterinary support when needed.

Outdoor Activities

Daily walks and playtime in safe outdoor environments.
