About Us

Loving Homes Dog Hotel was created with one simple goal: to provide a safe, comfortable and caring environment where dogs feel truly at home. We understand that leaving your pet behind can be stressful, which is why we focus on building trust with both dogs and their owners. Our services are designed to meet different needs, combining professional care with a warm and welcoming atmosphere to ensure every dog enjoys their stay.

Why We Started Loving Homes

Loving Homes was founded from a genuine passion for animals and a desire to create a better experience for both dogs and their owners. We recognised that many pet care services focus only on basic needs, often overlooking comfort, emotional wellbeing and personalised care. As dog owners ourselves, we understood how important it is to know that your pet is not just safe, but also happy and well cared for. This inspired us to build a space that goes beyond traditional boarding. Our aim was to create a place where dogs are treated with attention, patience and understanding, while owners feel confident and reassured. Every aspect of Loving Homes has been designed to reflect these values, combining high-quality care with a welcoming and stress-free environment.

The Problem We Solve

Many dog owners struggle to find reliable and trustworthy care for their pets when they are away. Traditional boarding options often focus only on basic needs, with limited attention given to comfort, safety and emotional wellbeing. This can lead to stress for both the dog and the owner, as there is uncertainty about the quality of care being provided. Loving Homes addresses this issue by offering a professional and caring environment where dogs are treated with attention and respect. Our services are designed to go beyond basic boarding by providing structured routines, safe facilities and personalised care tailored to each dog’s needs. This ensures that pets remain comfortable, active and well looked after, while owners have peace of mind knowing their dog is in a secure and supportive environment.

Our Vision

Our vision at Loving Homes is to become a trusted and recognised provider of high-quality dog care, known for combining professionalism with genuine compassion. We aim to set a standard where pet care goes beyond basic services, focusing on comfort, safety and emotional wellbeing. As we grow, we hope to expand our facilities and services while maintaining the same level of personalised care that defines our brand. Our goal is to create a space where every dog feels secure and valued, and every owner feels confident leaving their pet in our care.

Find Us in Hong Kong

Loving Homes Dog Hotel is based in Hong Kong, in a calm and secure environment designed specifically for high-quality pet care. Our location provides easy access for clients across the city while ensuring a peaceful setting away from busy urban stress, helping dogs feel relaxed and comfortable during their stay.
