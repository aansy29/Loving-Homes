Our Facilities

At Loving Homes, our facilities are designed to provide a safe, comfortable and engaging environment for every dog. We focus on creating spaces where pets can relax, play and feel secure, ensuring their physical and emotional wellbeing is always prioritised. From open play areas to comfortable resting spaces, each facility has been carefully designed to meet different needs and activity levels.

Outdoor Paddocks

Our outdoor paddocks provide a safe and spacious environment where dogs can exercise, explore and socialise freely. Designed with secure fencing and soft ground surfaces, these areas allow pets to stay active while remaining fully supervised. The paddocks support both group play and individual time, ensuring that dogs of all energy levels feel comfortable and engaged. This space helps promote physical health, mental stimulation and overall wellbeing during their stay.
